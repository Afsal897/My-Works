### Project Title: Employee Management System (EMS)

#### Core Features:
1. **User Authentication**:
   - Registration and login for employees, managers, and admin roles.
   - Role-based access control.

2. **Dashboard**:
   - Overview of total employees.
   - Active employees in projects.
   - Employees on the bench (not assigned to any project).
   - Employees on notice period.
   - Each item can redirect to a detailed list of corresponding employees.

3. **Project Assignment**:
   - Admin/Manager can assign employees to projects.
   - Maintain a list of ongoing projects with assigned resources, due dates, and descriptions.

4. **Employee Self-Service Portal**:
   - Employees can view details about their assigned projects, including due date, description, and team members.
   - Employees can manage the technologies used in their projects and update their skills.

5. **Team Collaboration**:
   - View teammates from various departments involved in the projects.
   - Option for employees to provide feedback on teammates.

6. **Performance Rating**:
   - Managers can rate employee performance based on project contributions.
   - Employees receive notifications regarding their performance ratings.

7. **Reporting**:
   - Generate reports on employee performance, project status, and resource allocation.
   - Admin can view overall statistics and insights about employee distribution.


6. **Implement Additional Features**:
   - Add functionality for rating employees and sending notifications.
   - Develop the employee self-service portal.

7. **Testing**:
   - Conduct testing for functionality, performance, and security.
   - Implement user acceptance testing to ensure usability.

8. **Deployment**:
   - Deploy the application to a cloud platform.
   - Ensure proper domain setup and SSL configuration for security.

9. **Maintenance and Updates**:
   - Monitor the application and gather user feedback.
   - Iterate on the application by adding new features or improving existing ones.
